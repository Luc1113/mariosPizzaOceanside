About Mario's Pizza

Mario's Pizza has been a cornerstone of the Oceanside community since 1982. For over 40 years, we've been serving authentic Italian pizza and Italian-American cuisine to our neighbors in Oceanside, Baldwin, East Rockaway, and Rockville Centre.

Our family-owned pizzeria takes pride in using traditional recipes and fresh ingredients. Every pizza is made to order, ensuring that each slice meets the high standards our customers have come to expect over the decades.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick slice, ordering dinner for the family, or catering your next event, we're here to serve you with the same quality and care that has kept us in business for over four decades.
